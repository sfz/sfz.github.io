## Examples

```
ampeg_attack=1.2
fileg_attack=0.1
```

These are very frequently used, especially with amplifier envelopes.
`ampeg_attack` is the standard "A" in the basic ADSR volume envelope.
`fileg_attack` is key to 303-style basses.
