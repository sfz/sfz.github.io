Can be quite useful for making vibrato sound more realistic on instruments such
as bowed strings, usually combined with [lfoN_delay].

## Examples

```sfz
lfo01_fade=0.1
lfo02_fade=1.2
lfo02_fade_oncc21=2.5
```


[lfoN_delay]: lfoN_delay.md
